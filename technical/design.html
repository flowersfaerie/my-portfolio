Tools of the Trade

In the world of graphic design and desktop publishing, there are only a few really good programs that have become the industry standard. Of these, Adobe Create Cloud is recognized as among the best. Within this suite of programs can be found Photoshop, Indesign, and numerous others which are used by industry professionals around the world. Over the course of the last few years, I have pursued a course teaching myself how to use these programs in order to develop beautifully laid out document for the enjoyment of those who may view them. Below you will find a selection of the various documents that I have created using these programs.

TVEN Quarterly Magazine

As the Founder and Executive Director of the Transgender Visibility & Education Network (TVEN), I decided to craft a quarterly magazine (using Indesign & Photoshop) which would serve the needs of the transgender community and our supporters. I served as both the graphic designer, primary writer, and Senior Editor of the magazine during its five-issue run.
