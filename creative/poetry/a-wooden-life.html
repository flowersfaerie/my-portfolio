A Wooden Life

A poem (Published: March 2013 & March 2016)

Cut down, stolen
from home. Family left
behind, decades
meaningless. Wooden
giants, remnants before
sawdust. Pieces
chipped away, shaped
to mold. Kicked
around, blocking
progression. Wedged. Meaning
of life, door stopped.
