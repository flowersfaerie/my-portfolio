Creative Portfolio

Fiction. Poetry. Calligraphy

Fiction

From early childhood until the present day, I have always had a passion for stories and storytelling. I remember reading The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings for the first time -- for fun -- when I was seven years old. These stories and thousands of others like them have inspired me to craft stories of my own. While many of the stories I have started are still considered works in progress, within the pages of this Creative Arts Portfolio I am happy to share with you a few of the stories I have worked on over the years.

Poetry

While I do not specfically consider myself to be a poet, I have thoroughly enjoyed the fine art of crafting poems. I have even been honored to have had a few of my poems published over the years. Herein, I am proud to share all of my poetry with you.

Calligraphy

Having witnessed the majestic elegance of some of history's most beautiful illuminated manuscripts, it is very easy to understand why the written word, in the form of calligraphy, has held a special place in my heart. In 2011, I decided to try teachning myself how to write in this time honored style. To date, nearly every piece of calligraphic art that I have create I have given away as gifts. That said, I did plan ahead by photographing most of those pieces so that I could share them with you here.
