Graphic Design

As a word artist (see my Creative Portfolio), I have found myself naturally drawn to how words and phrases are organized on a page or within a document. As a matter of necessity and great interest, I set out to teach myself how to use craft images and documents using the Adobe Creative Cloud set of programs. The files in this section were designed utilizing Adobe's InDesign and Photoshop.

Web Development

Starting in October 2019, I have begun a Full Stack Web Development program at Lambda School> (expected completion: mid-2021). While I am still in the middle of the learning program, my experience with crafting and working with the design and development of websites stretches back to the early years of the fledgling world wide web (circa mid-1990s). Those early web design project were crafted entirely with HTML, which I taught myself. Since then, I have been learning how to design and develop websites utilizing HTML, CSS (including the LESS preprocessor), Javascript, and am stretching further into REACT and other development languages. This section contains websites that I have developed utilizing these technologies.
